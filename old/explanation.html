How It Works

  • The Square Footage of all the bedrooms is added up then subtracted from the Total Square Footage
  • This leaves the Common Space of the floor plan.
    • Ex: 1000sqFt total - 550sqFt total of rooms = 450sqFt Common Space
  • Then the % of Common Space compared to Total Footage is found.
    • Ex: 450sqFt common space / 1000sqFt total = 45% common space
  • Multiplying Rent by the common space % gives the value of the Common Space.
    • Ex: $1000 Rent * 45% = $450 value for the Common Space
  • That value is then divided equally by the number of Occupants giving the base rent.
    • Ex: $450 / 5 Occupants = $90 base rent per occupant
  • The Common Space rent is subtracted from total rent giving the value of private space.
    • Ex: $1000 Rent - $450 = $550 value for the Private Space
  • The SqFt of each room is dividied by the remaining footage to find its % of private space.
    • Ex: 110sqFt room / 550sqFt private space = 20% of Total Private Space
  • The % of Private Space is divided by Occupants in that room to find each individuals %.
    • Ex: 20% / 2 Occupants = 10% Private Space for each person in that room
  • Each person's % private space is multiplied by the remaining rent to get their Private Payment
    • Ex: 10% * $550 remaining rent = $55 Private Payment
  • Each person's Private Payment is added to the Base Payment to get their Total Payment
    • Ex: $90 Base Payment + $55 Private Payment = $145 Total Payment for that individual
